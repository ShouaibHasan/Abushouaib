Research

Undergrad Thesis

Analysis of Electrical Safety and Risk Index in Ready-Made Garment Factories of Bangladesh

This study aims to describe the electrical safety of RMG factories based on compliance with the questionnaire with the support of Accord, Alliance, and DIFE. Descriptive statistics analysis was performed to obtain centralization, variation, and distribution of maximum precipitation data. Cluster analysis classifies Electrical hazards into two kinds- Fire Risk and another is Shock hazard.
